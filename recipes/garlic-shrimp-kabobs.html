Garlic Shrimp Kabobs

Flavourful shrimp, perfect as part of a summer party. Very easy to make, the longest part is leaving them to marinate. They cook up in 5 minutes so they are the perfect snack to tide guests over before the main event at a dinner party.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Frozen Srimp, thawed and peeled
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 1 pinch finely chopped Parsley
  • Metal Skewers
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Place the shrimp into a bowl with the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, pepper, parsley and mix together. Leave to marinate for 2 hours.
  2. Coat metal skewers with cooking spray and pre-heat the grill.
  3. Place about 5 shrimp onto each skewer.
  4. Cook on grill, turning regularly for about 5 minutes. Do not overcook.
