Baked Tomato Slices

A perfectly versatile side dish. It can be paired with light dishes such as fish for a summery dish, or with something heavier such as lamb for a nice winter meal to warm you up.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil, as needed
  • 1 Large Tomato, cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices
  • 1 Fresh Sprig of Rosemary, leaves stripped and finely chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, minced
  • Salt and Ground Pepper to taste
