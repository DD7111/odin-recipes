Victoria Sponge

A moist vanilla sponge cake, with a layer of buttercream and jam sandwiched in between. The perfect cake for any occasion, and very simple to make! You can even use this recipe as a starting point to experiment with different flavours or different methods of decorating.

Ingredients

For the Sponge:

  • 200g Self Raising Flour
  • 200g Unsalted Butter
  • 200g Caster Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • A Few Drops of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 circular baking tins

For the Filling:

  • 150g Unsalted Butter
  • 200g Icing Sugar
  • Good Quality Jam

Steps:

  1. Bring the butter to room temperature, then cream it together with the sugar.
  2. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Add your vanilla extract
  3. Fold in the flour
  4. Line and grease your baking tins, then divide the mixture between the two
  5. Bake in the oven at 180 degrees celsius.
  6. Once cooked, turn out onto a rack to cool
  7. While cakes are cooling, beat together the icing sugar and butter for the buttercream filling.
  8. Once cooled, spread buttercream on one half and jam on the other, then place two halves together to assemble the cake.
  9. Dust with icing sugar to finish, the cake will keep for about 5 days.
